About the Branch

Nestled within the JW Marriott Hotel New Delhi Aerocity, Green Cuisine, affectionately known as "The Green Place" by locals, offers a transformative vegan dining experience. We've taken inspiration from around the globe to craft a menu bursting with innovative and flavorful plant-based creations.

Green Cuisine is more than just a restaurant; it's a haven for discerning vegans seeking a culinary adventure. Whether you're a local resident or a traveler exploring the wonders near Indira Gandhi International Airport, we invite you to savor the finest vegan cuisine in Aerocity.

From soul-satisfying breakfasts to vibrant lunches and delectable dinners, our menu caters to every craving. We champion fresh, seasonal ingredients, transforming them into dishes that are both delicious and nourishing. Join us at Green Cuisine and discover a world of flavor, where every bite is a testament to our passion for plant-based excellence.

Please note: Valet parking is available at the JW Marriott Hotel New Delhi Aerocity for a fee.
